About Me

Hi, I'm Kenneth. I'm a frontend developer, multimedia artist, and campus journalist from Dapitan City. I'm a Senior High School graduate of Dapitan City National High School, where I spent most of my time building custom websites from the ground up.

Aside from coding, I do a lot of digital storytelling and live broadcasting. I'm the Former Chief Copy Editor for Insight PH, and I recently represented our school as a regional qualifier for the Regional Schools Press Conference (RSPC) in Region 9. Writing and editing have really helped me learn how to get my ideas across clearly.

I also hold an NCII certification. When I'm not typing code, you'll probably find me holding a camera, running the control room for a live broadcast, editing videos, or playing Sepak Takraw.

For me, a good project isn't just about code that works perfectly—it has to look great and be easy for people to use. I'm always trying out new tools and learning as much as I can to keep improving my skills in both frontend development and UI design.
